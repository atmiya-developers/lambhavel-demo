Gangadhara Primary School

Gangadhara Primary School was established in 1936 and it is managed by the Local body. It is located in Rural area. It is located in Palsana block of Surat district of Gujarat. The school consists of Grades from 1 to 8. The school is Co-educational and it doesn't have an attached pre-primary section. Gujarati is the medium of instructions in this school. This school is approachable by all weather road. In this school academic session starts in April
